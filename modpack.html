Modpack

For The Horde

The Horde has some extra features while used with the custom modpack, including:

Visually Improved Zombies

Due to the technical limits of vanilla-compatible servers, zombies in The Horde while using a vanilla client look largely the same apart from custom heads. When playing with the modpack, they gain varied appearances, disturbing sounds, and semi-realistic animations.


Voice Chat

Text chat in Minecraft can feel clunky, slow, and not immersive. The implementation of voice chat hopes to negate all three of these issues. Voice chat can interact with the world, most notably sculk sensors and the warden. Sounds from voice chat will bounce and echo realistically, as well as all other sounds made (requires modpack, of course).


Irrelevant render distance

When using the modpack, your render distance becomes irrelevant, as chunks will be rendered extremely far out. Unlike most mods that do this, chunks do not have to be explored to appear while not in them. This is because the server will automatically send chunks to your client, providing maximum immersion and ease of exploration.


Extra Features

The Modpack also provides some small features that help with immersion. These include small pieces of immersion, the ability to move with your inventory open, VR support, a custom main menu, shaders, custom emotes, tools appearing on your back, and lots of tiny others.
