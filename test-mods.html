Do you want to help the server release ahead of schedule, as well as ensure it's as good as possible? You can, by playtesting some of the mods that will be used on the server.


The Horde is a modded Minecraft zombie apocalypse server designed to be compatible with vanilla clients, but still offering a very modded and nonvanilla experience. Many of the mods only work in multiplayer, or have functionality intended for it. This means that I, as a single person, can't do all the testing myself. And as many people are eager to get their hands on some of the mods, I figured you all can do some playtesting. Most of the testing sections also have a channel in the discord.

Test Progression

If you want to test the custom progression and its changes to the vanilla game (and give feedback), please submit a request in #test-zombies in the discord(preferably), or email me at thenewelskeptico@gmail.com with "zombie testing" in the subject line.


Test Zombies

If you want to test the custom zombies and their behavior (and give feedback), please submit a request in #test-zombies in the discord(preferably), or email me at thenewelskeptico@gmail.com with "zombie testing" in the subject line.


Test Equipment

If you want to test the custom equipment (guns, explosives, etc.) (and give feedback), please submit a request in #test-equipment in the discord(preferably), or email me at thenewelskeptico@gmail.com with "equipment testing" in the subject line.
